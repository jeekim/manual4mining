# Mining Identifiers

### Why mining identifiers?

* They are almost unique.
* Once mined, metadata can be fetched.
* Can provide links between identifiers

### What are identifiers?

* Accession numbers
* DOIs
* URLs
* Mobile numbers
* E-mail addresses

### Problem definition

* An information extraction problem
